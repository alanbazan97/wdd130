Rafting Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to establish an online presence for a water rafting company. The website will provide information about the company's services, including rafting trips, equipment rentals, and safety guidelines. The website will also allow customers to book trips and purchase equipment online.

Audience

The target audience for this water rafting website is adventure-seeking individuals and groups, aged 18-35, with a moderate to high income, who enjoy outdoor activities and are interested in trying new experiences. The website will also appeal to families with children over the age of 10 who are looking for a unique and exciting vacation experience.

Scenario questions:

  • Who are the target customers?
    • The target customers are adventure-seeking individuals and groups, aged 18-35, with a moderate to high income, who enjoy outdoor activities and are interested in trying new experiences. The website will also appeal to families with children over the age of 10 who are looking for a unique and exciting vacation experience.
  • What do they want?
    • The customers want to have a thrilling and memorable experience while rafting on the water. They also want to feel safe and secure throughout their trip and have access to quality equipment and services.
  • What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
    • The customers expect to find information about the company's services, including rafting trips, equipment rentals, and safety guidelines. They also expect to be able to book trips and purchase equipment online, as well as read reviews and testimonials from previous customers.
