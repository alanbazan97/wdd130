Rafting Site Plan

Alan Ronaldo Bazan Monge

Overview

Purpose

Rafting Site will be a place for water rafting enthusiasts to gather and share experiences about this hobby. There will be trainings and forums dedicated to white water skills development, techniques, products and locations. The site will be financially supported by advertising and product sell on the site. The products are all intended to help the customer in their rafting experience.

Audience

Rafting Site will have a focus to anyone who has an interest in rafting, helping the beginners in their first steps into the rafting experience.
