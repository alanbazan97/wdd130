Trip 1

The "Big Drops" adventure: A thrilling white water rafting trip that takes you through a series of challenging rapids, including Class III and IV. The trip covers 12 miles and includes stunning scenery and wildlife sightings.

Trip 2

The "Extreme Expedition": A multi-day white water rafting trip that takes you through some of the most remote and challenging rapids in the country. This trip covers over 50 miles and includes Class IV and V rapids, as well as camping under the stars and breathtaking views of the wilderness.

Trip 3

The "Family Fun" trip: A milder white water rafting adventure that is perfect for families with young children or those looking for a more relaxed experience. This trip covers 8 miles and includes Class II and III rapids, as well as opportunities for swimming and picnicking.
